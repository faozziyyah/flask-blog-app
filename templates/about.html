Our History

Started in 2020, EduBlog quickly established itself as a curative icon par excellence in Nigeria. With its unique brand of world fusion writing that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele in Nigeria.

The Blog traces its humble beginnings to The Golden Pen, a successful chain started by our CEO, Mr. Peter George, that featured for the first time the world's best Times.
